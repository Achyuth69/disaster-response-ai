HYDERABAD GEOGRAPHIC AND INFRASTRUCTURE DATA
============================================

DISTRICT OVERVIEW
-----------------
Hyderabad is divided into 16 districts across the Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC).
Key districts prone to flooding: Khairatabad, Secunderabad, LB Nagar, Serilingampally, Kukatpally.

MAJOR ROADS AND CONNECTIVITY
-----------------------------
NH-44 (National Highway 44): Connects Hyderabad to Nagpur. Passes through Secunderabad.
  - Flood risk: HIGH near Musi River crossings at Chaderghat and Nayapul bridges.
  - Elevation: 505–520m above sea level.

NH-65 (National Highway 65): Hyderabad–Pune corridor.
  - Flood risk: MEDIUM near Patancheru industrial area.

Outer Ring Road (ORR): 158km ring road encircling the city.
  - Flood risk: LOW (elevated construction), but underpasses flood at 50mm/hr rainfall.

Inner Ring Road (IRR): Connects all major city zones.
  - Flood risk: HIGH at Mehdipatnam, Tolichowki, and Masab Tank underpasses.

BRIDGES AND CRITICAL CROSSINGS
--------------------------------
Chaderghat Bridge (Musi River): Critical crossing. Closes when water level > 7m.
Nayapul Bridge (Musi River): Closes when water level > 6.5m.
Purana Pul (Old Bridge, Musi River): Heritage structure. Closes when water level > 5m.
Attapur Bridge: Connects south Hyderabad. Flood risk HIGH.
Nagole Bridge: East Hyderabad. Moderate flood risk.

WATER BODIES
-------------
Hussain Sagar Lake: Central lake. Capacity 15.5 MCM. Overflow risk when level > 513.5m.
Musi River: Flows east-west through city. Critical flood trigger at 7m gauge level.
Osman Sagar (Gandipet): Reservoir. Capacity 3.7 TMC. Releases increase downstream risk.
Himayat Sagar: Reservoir. Capacity 2.97 TMC.
Mir Alam Tank: Urban lake. Overflow affects Mehdipatnam.
Saroornagar Lake: Affects LB Nagar and Saroornagar areas.

EVACUATION ROUTES
------------------
Primary: NH-44 northbound to Secunderabad Railway Station (capacity: 50,000 persons/hour)
Secondary: ORR westbound to Patancheru relief camps
Tertiary: NH-65 southbound to Shamshabad airport relief zone
Emergency: Elevated Metro Rail corridors (pedestrian evacuation only)
